About Us

What We Do

We make simple, unbiased pregnancy planning tools for women of all backgrounds.


Why We Do It

After having a child, we experienced how overwhelming planning a pregnancy can be. We wanted to compile all the resources we found in one place.


Our Passions

We believe every woman, regardless of race, background, or socioeconomic status, deserves the ability to take control of her pregnancy.


Credibility

myHoneyBump was created by nurses. We try our very best to use credible resources for our content. All of our resources are available to you for further reading.
