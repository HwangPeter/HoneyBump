About Us

What is myHoneyBump?


myHoneyBump is a website that offers free pregnancy planning tools to women going through pregnancy.


Why do we do it?


Pregnancy is an exciting time in a couple's life but can also become overwhelming with how much there is to know and do. Being an advocate for women's health, we see an opportunity to improve the pregnancy experience. Our goal at myHoneyBump is to collect information from credible sources to make simple, unbiased, pregnancy planning tools for women of all backgrounds.
