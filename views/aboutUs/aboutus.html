About Us

What we do

We make simple, unbiased pregnancy planning tools for women of all backgrounds.


Why we do it

After having a child, we experienced how overwhelming planning a pregnancy can be. We wanted to compile all the resources we found in one place and present it in a manageable way.


Our passions

We believe every woman, regardless of race, background, or socioeconomic status, deserves the ability to take control of her pregnancy.


Mission Statement

my Honeybump improves the peripartum experience for women of all backgrounds by simplifying pregnancy health and utilizing technology to provide personalized, credible pregnancy planning tools.
